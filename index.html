For 1000 years, the Order of Light has defended the Mirror Tower, home of the Amulet of Light. This powerful artifact allows the wielder to control the cycle of night and day. For these 1000 years, the Order has kept the Amulet out of the hands of vampires, beasts of the night out to make it so it never becomes light in the land again. Of recent, their evil plans are close to reality, with them gaining access to the Mirror Tower. You are the Seal Bearer, one of few blessed individuals who wield an amazing iron will and tough body. Make your way to the Order's remnant. Take the Tower back, and save the land.
